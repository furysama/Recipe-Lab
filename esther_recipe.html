Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins (With Heath Bits)

I tend to bake when I need something to do that keeps me busy and helps to work off stress. It is nice to listen to a podcast, do the methodical tasks of ingredient prep and mixing, and appear to be doing something busy and important so that the kids ask dad for help instead of me for a while! It helps if they want to eat the end results. And I don't mind the bonus of having a treat at the end, either!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups of flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup mashed frozen or fresh bananas (about 3)
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cups melted butter
  • ½ cups chocolate chips
  • ½ cups heath bits

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line muffin pan with reusable silicone liners.
  2. Combine baking soda, salt and flour in a small bowl and stir in the chocolate chips and heath.
  3. Combine sugar, mashed bananas, egg, butter and vanilla in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet, and mix until just combined.
  5. Pour mixture into prepared muffin tin and bake for 18-20 minutes until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Try not to overbake!
  6. Let cool and then enjoy!
